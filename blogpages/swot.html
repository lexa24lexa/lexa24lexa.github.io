SWOT Analysis

Strenghts

As strengths I'm encouraging, very persistent, even more curious, taste for learning new things and I'm very organized.

Weaknesses

I'm a very stubborn person, I'm too proud and sometimes it gets in my way and I'm very easy to get upset.

Opportunities

Being encouraging makes me a good person for leadership and being a patient person also helps.

As I'm a stubborn person means that I don't give up easily. When I have an idea I will go all the way until I get what I want.

Threats

My biggest threat is my pride. I find it difficult to recognize when other people are right because I like to have the stuff done my way.

I also have a difficult time getting my pride down in front of other people to to fix or solve problems that I didn't cause.

I'm doing my best to work on this and get this very big aspect in me solved.
