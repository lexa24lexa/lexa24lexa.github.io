This is my blog page that has all of the important information about me as a IT Professional.

ICT field of work

When?

i've been interested in IT ever since i was little. i've always tried to find out more about it and whenever i could, i'd go and help my family when they had problems with their computer or cell phone.

How?

in my last year of high school i had a young IT teacher and, in one of my first lessons with him, he asked if i was interested in the area and i said yes but i didn't know where to start or how to invest in it.

he suggested i take some courses at FreeCodeCamp to see if i liked it and it quickly became something of a hobby that i did after school.

Why?

i realized that i really like programming and that it's something i can imagine myself doing in the future, mainly because i like puzzles and ICT is a huge puzzle. i can be creative and, at the same time, please those I work with.
